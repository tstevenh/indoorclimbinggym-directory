## What Climbro Says the Product Is

Across the official site, Climbro describes itself as:

- a smart hangboard
- a system for training and measuring climbing-specific strength
- a product with guided app testing and training

On the Mini page, Climbro also highlights:

- lightweight and travel-friendly
- 6 hold sizes from 1.3 cm to 4 cm
- warm-up, rehab, testing, and training use cases

That gives the clearest public picture of what Climbro is offering right now.

## What Climbro Says the Training Process Looks Like

On the official `How it works` page, Climbro says the process starts with a **Performance Test Battery** made up of four tests:

- Max Strength
- Finger RFD
- Continuous
- Intermittent

Climbro says those tests provide insight into key physical qualities of the forearms and are presented as climbing grades in a radar chart.

The same page says:

- the tests should be done when fully recovered
- the battery can be completed over several days
- all four tests should be finished within a maximum of two weeks
- the battery results are valid for one month

## What Climbro Says About Training Plans

Climbro's `How it works` page says that after the battery, users can get a personal training plan to improve their weakest point.

Climbro says the plan:

- consists of 16 to 24 training sessions
- includes exercises with different intensity, repetitions, work times, and rest times
- is tracked by the app
- guides users on when to train or rest

The page also says users can repeat the Performance Test Battery after finishing the current training plan to check progress and get a new plan.

## What Climbro Says About the App

Climbro's FAQ says:

- you do not need a subscription to use the hangboard
- the free app includes Max-Strength Tests, Single Exercises, Logs, Analytics, and more
- the Performance Test Battery and Personal Training Plan are premium features

That is an important part of the official value proposition. Climbro is not only selling hardware. The official site also sells the testing and plan workflow inside the app.

## What Climbro Says About Lower-Risk Buying Options

Climbro currently publishes two lower-risk offer paths on the official site:

### Rental

The rent page says:

- no commitment
- cancel or buy out at any time
- free returns

### 30-Day Home Trial

The 30-Day Home Trial page says:

- users can test Climbro for the first 30 days after delivery
- the first 30 days can be used to complete the Performance Test Battery
- users can assess their current form and create a tailored training plan
- if Climbro is not the right match, Climbro offers a return process and full refund
