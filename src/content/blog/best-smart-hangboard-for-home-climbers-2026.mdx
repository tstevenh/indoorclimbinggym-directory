## What Counts as a Smart Hangboard?

This matters because a lot of products in this space sound similar but are not solving the same job.

A **true smart hangboard** is a hangboard or hangboard-like hardware product with a built-in digital training layer. That usually means app connection, structured workouts, automated tracking, or integrated testing.

A **smart hangboard alternative** is different. It may be a force plate, force sensor, or training device that gives you better data, but it is not itself a full hangboard system.

That is why this article separates the market into two groups:

- **direct smart hangboards:** Climbro, Zlagboard
- **smart alternatives:** Entralpi, Tindeq, PitchSix

If you blur those together, the roundup gets longer but the buying advice gets worse.

## FAQ

### What is the best smart hangboard for home climbers?

For most home climbers, Climbro is the strongest overall smart hangboard system because it combines board hardware, guided testing, training plans, and tracking in one workflow.

### Is Climbro better than Zlagboard?

Climbro looks better if you want a more complete testing-and-training ecosystem. Zlagboard looks better if you mainly want an app-connected hangboard with automated hangs and workouts.

### Do I need a smart hangboard if I already own a hangboard?

Not necessarily. If you already own a good hangboard, a smart alternative like Entralpi or Tindeq may make more sense than replacing your hardware.

### Is Entralpi a smart hangboard?

No. Entralpi is better described as a smart force plate for hangboard training, not a full smart hangboard.

### Is Tindeq a hangboard?

No. Tindeq Progressor is a Bluetooth force sensor used for testing and no-hang style training workflows.

### What is the difference between a smart hangboard and a normal hangboard?

A normal hangboard is mainly just hardware. A smart hangboard adds a digital training layer such as app connection, guided workouts, automated tracking, testing, or progress feedback.
