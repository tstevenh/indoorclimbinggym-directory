## The 8-Week Rule (Simple and Brutal)

Before overthinking discounts, commit to this:
- 2 sessions/week
- for 8 weeks
- log each completed session

At week 8:
- 80%+ adherence → buying usually makes sense
- under 80% → keep risk low with rent/trial

This isn’t sexy. It works.

## Common Mistakes When Chasing Climbro Deals
