If you want the short version, Climbro currently presents three official paths on its site:

- `Rent`: the rent page says "no commitment," "cancel or buy out at any time," and "free returns."
- `Buy New`: the Climbro shop states first-time purchases of a new Climbro hangboard include a 30-day home trial.
- `Buy Refurbished`: the Climbro shop says refurbished boards come from the 30-day trial program, are inspected and repaired, and are not eligible for the 30-day trial or returns after delivery.

This article keeps to those official claims only.

## Rent: What Climbro's Official Page Says

The current Climbro rent page describes the rental offer this way:

- no commitment
- cancel at any time
- buy out at any time
- free returns

That is the clearest low-commitment path currently described on the official site.

## Buy New: What Climbro's Official Shop Says

On Climbro's shop and FAQ sections, the new-purchase path includes:

- a 30-day risk-free home trial for first-time purchases of a new Climbro hangboard
- a 24-month warranty

Climbro's FAQ says the 30-day home trial starts when the board arrives. If the product is not a match, Climbro says customers can contact them within 30 days to schedule pickup and receive a refund.

## Buy Refurbished: What Climbro's Official Shop Says

Climbro's FAQ says refurbished hangboards:

- are returned boards from the 30-day trial program
- are inspected, repaired, and tested to meet Climbro standards
- may have slight cosmetic damage such as minor scratches
- are covered by the same 24-month warranty as new boards
- are not eligible for the 30-day home trial
- are not returnable after delivery

If you are comparing official purchase paths, this is the main difference to keep in mind: the trial and return language is tied to first-time purchases of new boards, not refurbished boards.

## What Is Included With a Rental?

Climbro's FAQ says a rental includes:

- a Climbro hangboard
- access to all premium features of the Climbro app
- a full warranty for the duration of the rental agreement

That is the main official distinction between the rental path and a standard purchase page. The rental offer is framed around lower commitment and bundled premium app access.

## Do You Need an App Subscription?

Climbro's FAQ says:

- you do **not** need a subscription to use the Climbro hangboard
- the free app includes Max-Strength Tests, Single Exercises, Logs, Analytics, and more
- the **Performance Test Battery** and **Personal Training Plan** are premium features

That matters when comparing rent vs buy, because the rental FAQ specifically says premium app features are included with rental.

## What the 30-Day Home Trial Page Adds

Climbro's 30-Day Home Trial page adds a few more official details:

- Climbro encourages users to use the first 30 days to complete the Performance Test Battery
- the page says users can assess their current climbing form, identify areas to improve, and create a tailored training plan
- if Climbro is not the right match, the page says the return process is simple and includes a full refund
- Climbro asks users to keep the original packaging during the trial period

## Final Take

If you want the most flexible path described on Climbro's official site, the rental page is the clearest no-commitment option.

If you want ownership and Climbro's official trial policy, the new-purchase path is the one that includes the 30-Day Home Trial.
